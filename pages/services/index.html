My Core Services

I support digital product teams in building accessible, usable interfaces — through consulting, coaching, and hands-on frontend implementation.

I help digital product teams integrate web accessibility into their everyday workflows — sustainably, pragmatically, and without slowing down delivery. With a 10-year background as a frontend developer specialized in accessibility and a Scrum Master certification, I support companies in embedding accessibility across the entire product lifecycle: from discovery and design to development, QA, and release. My work focuses on building internal competence, aligning accessibility with agile ways of working, and helping teams move from one-off fixes to long-term accessibility maturity.

Service 1: Accessibility in the Product Lifecycle

I work with teams to introduce accessibility early and consistently, rather than treating it as a final compliance step.

This includes:

  • integrating accessibility into discovery, requirements, and acceptance criteria
  • aligning accessibility practices with Scrum and agile workflows
  • clarifying roles, responsibilities, and ownership within teams
  • supporting product, engineering, design, and content stakeholders

Service 2: Hands-on Coaching & Enablement

Accessibility works best when teams understand how to implement it themselves.

I support teams through:

  • coaching and mentoring frontend developers on accessible implementation
  • accessibility guidance for UX/UI designers
  • support for content and copy teams (inclusive language, alt text, captions, transcripts)
  • reviews, feedback, and practical examples tailored to the product

Service 3: Hands-on Implementation & Embedded Support

Depending on team needs, I support accessibility work hands-on.

Hands-on work is always combined with knowledge transfer, so accessibility skills stay in the team long after my engagement ends.

This can include:

  • frontend accessibility implementation
  • component and pattern reviews
  • pairing with developers
  • temporary embedded support within product teams

Frontend Development

In addition to accessibility consulting, I also take on frontend development work — particularly when accessibility, quality, and collaboration with product teams are central.

Typical work includes:

  • frontend development for product or agency teams
  • implementation of accessible UI components and patterns
  • improving existing codebases with a focus on usability and accessibility
  • project-based or time-bound support
